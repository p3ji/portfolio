# Project Workflow

This project follows the **Decide-Execute-Deliver** framework to ensure alignment, quality, and maintainability. This structure places the most critical thinking at the top of the process.

## 1. Decide (The Top)
*The most critical phase where direction is set. We may jump back here if requirements shift.*
- **Understand Requirements**: Clarify the feature, design aesthetic, and user experience goals.
- **Research**: Analyze existing code, design systems, and best practices.
- **Implementation Plan**: Propose a concrete plan (`implementation_plan.md`) outlining the files to change and the approach.
- **Human Approval**: The software engineer (human) reviews the plan, answers open questions, and gives the green light.

## 2. Execute (The Middle)
*The rapid implementation phase where the AI assists with heavy lifting.*
- **Draft Code**: Write the HTML, CSS, and JavaScript as outlined in the plan.
- **Track Progress**: Maintain a task list (`task.md`) to ensure all planned components are addressed.
- **Component Assembly**: Build reusable pieces and integrate them into the main application.

## 3. Deliver (The Bottom)
*Verification and accountability.*
- **Verify**: Ensure the code runs correctly, the design is responsive, and aesthetics meet premium standards.
- **Walkthrough**: Summarize what was built (`walkthrough.md`) for final review.
- **Accountability**: The human engineer reviews the final output, ensuring it meets the business and technical needs before deployment.
