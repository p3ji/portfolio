My approach

As a sociologist, economist, and on-leave manager in the public service, I view artificial intelligence not just as an engineering marvel, but as fundamentally shifting how work is organized individually and within groups at every level. As a long-time observer of technology and AI’s economic and societal impact, I am taking some dedicated time to explore more closely the capabilities and possibilities of this quickly evolving technology. To do this, I am building functional prototypes, experimenting with new workflows, and analyzing how AI can transform organizational operations.

My initial focus is exploring how AI can be leveraged to build entirely new digital products, or optimize existing workflows to meet evolving needs. To do this, I am diving deep into the end-to-end AI stack—covering everything from data management and semantic metadata to orchestration frameworks, model optimization, application security, and AI product design. Ultimately, I am taking a 'learning by doing' approach, and I'm excited to share what I build along the way.
